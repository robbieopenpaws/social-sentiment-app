Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to delete your data from Social Sentiment Analytics and what data is removed.

Quick Action: To immediately delete all your data, email us at robbie@openpaws.ai with your account email address.

1. What Data We Store

When you use our service, we store:

  • Your Facebook profile information (name, email)
  • Connected Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts
  • Public comments from your pages
  • Sentiment analysis results
  • Usage analytics and preferences

2. How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Through the Application

Step 1: Log into your Social Sentiment Analytics account at https://social-sentiment-app.vercel.app
Step 2: Go to Settings → Account Settings
Step 3: Click "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page
Step 4: Confirm deletion by typing "DELETE" in the confirmation box
Step 5: Click "Permanently Delete Account"

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to robbie@openpaws.ai with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you want to delete all data

We will process email requests within 48 hours.

3. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • ✅ Your profile and account information
  • ✅ All connected page data
  • ✅ Stored comments and posts
  • ✅ All sentiment analysis results
  • ✅ Usage history and preferences
  • ✅ Exported data files (if stored)

4. What We Keep (Legal Requirements)

For legal and security purposes, we may retain for up to 90 days:

  • Basic transaction logs (no personal content)
  • Security incident records
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

5. Facebook Data

Deleting your account from our service:

  • ✅ Removes all data we've collected from Facebook
  • ✅ Revokes our access to your Facebook pages
  • ❌ Does NOT delete your original Facebook content

Note: Your original Facebook posts and comments remain on Facebook. Only our copies and analysis are deleted.

6. Confirmation Process

After deletion:

  1. You'll receive an email confirmation within 24 hours
  2. Data deletion is completed within 48 hours
  3. You'll receive a final confirmation email
  4. Your account will be permanently inaccessible

7. Before You Delete

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider exporting your data first if you want to keep your analysis results.

To export your data before deletion:

  • Go to Export → Select "All Data"
  • Choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, Excel)
  • Download the file before deleting your account

8. Contact Support

If you have questions about data deletion:

  • Email: robbie@openpaws.ai
  • Website: https://social-sentiment-app.vercel.app
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours

9. Alternative: Temporary Deactivation

If you're unsure about permanent deletion, you can:

  • Disconnect all Facebook pages (keeps account active)
  • Pause data collection temporarily
  • Contact support for a temporary account freeze
