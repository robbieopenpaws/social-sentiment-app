Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Social Sentiment Analytics ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social media sentiment analysis service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Facebook Data

  • Profile Information: Basic profile information (name, email) for authentication
  • Page Access: Access to Facebook Pages you manage
  • Comments: Public comments on your Facebook Pages and Instagram posts
  • Posts: Public posts from your connected pages

2.2 Usage Data

  • Analytics and sentiment analysis results
  • Application usage statistics
  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Sentiment Analysis: Analyze sentiment in comments and posts
  • Dashboard Insights: Provide analytics and visualizations
  • Data Export: Allow you to export your analysis results
  • Service Improvement: Improve our algorithms and user experience

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Comments and posts: Up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Analysis results: Until you delete your account
  • Account information: Until account deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Facebook Integration

Our service integrates with Facebook to:

  • Authenticate users via Facebook Login
  • Access public comments on your managed pages
  • Retrieve public posts for analysis

We only access data you explicitly grant permission for and only from pages you manage.
